Hi! I am a final-year undergraduate student at the University of Toronto, major in Computer Engineering and minor in Artificial Intelligence. I am currently working on physically-based night-time image generation for autonomous driving at the Computer Vision Lab in ETH Zurich , supervisied by Prof. Luc Van Gool and Dr. Christos Sakaridis.

In the past, I was fortunate to work as a Software Development Engineer at Intel. I also worked at PAIR lab with Prof. Animesh Garg on Robotics and Computer Vision, at Forcolab with Prof. Shurui Zhou on Software Engineering, and at Computer Engineering Research Group with Prof. Paul Chow on Computer Networks and Security.
